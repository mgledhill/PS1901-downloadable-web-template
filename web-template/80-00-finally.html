This web site template is part of the Practical Series of Publications.

This section of the web template is intended to provide common information about the website: how to contact people, acknowledgements for the third party applications used within the website and legal and privacy information.

I’ve left my contact details in as a starting point; I would suggest you change these to reflect your own website requirements. The acknowledgment section is accurate and up to date; it reflects the third party software that comes with the web template.

The legal section is the typical set of disclaimers that come with websites these days; the licence is the MIT licence under which I’ve released this website.

The privacy and cookies section is accurate for the web template as it is issued; there are no cookies or any mechanisms for acquiring personal information within the template. You will need to modify this section if you add things like Google Analytics (see the legal and privacy section on my website for details of what I did) or other code that generates cookies.

Contact MG

You can reach me by email. I invite questions, corrections, constructive criticism and complaints (polite ones) with the following caveats:

  1. I do have a day job (surprising isn’t it), I will respond to all polite emails but not necessarily instantly.

  2. I can’t offer detailed engineering advice about specific problems (e.g. why does that valve blow all the fuses when I try to open it), but I will offer pearls of wisdom about less specific software issues.

  3. I don’t know anything about car engines or kettles so please don’t ask.

  4. For the more emotionally tainted, my dogs, Hector and Henry, are Salukis and while it may be off topic I have a soft spot for them and I may be willing to discuss them if pushed.

  5. If your email comes down to “I think your website’s rubbish, I won’t pay for it, but I do want to shout at you for a while about your outrageous shortcomings” then please, there is no need to trouble yourself; you’ve already said everything by not paying.

So if you’re happy with that, you can reach me here:

mg@practicalseries.com.

Privacy & cookies

This is a big thing now in Europe and in England: gdpr (General Data Protection Regulation); it means I have to be very careful with any data I collect about people. I also have to explain why I want the data and what I’m going to do with it. So here goes:

Cookies

This website template does not use nor does it install any cookies whatsoever.

In summary, you can browse this site with complete anonymity.

Privacy and personal data

In its downloadable form, this website template does not collect or ask for any personal data. There is no contact-me form requesting names, addresses, email details or phone numbers &c. Neither are there any user logons or other such forms of identification.
